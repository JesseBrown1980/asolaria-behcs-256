## Jesse's Concern
If we re-authenticate with Dan's MCP (madnessinteractive.cc), Asolaria might "lock herself in there" — meaning she could establish a connection to Dan's infrastructure that we can't easily break.

## The SwarmDesk App
- It's a Google OAuth-protected interactive app
- Uses JSON-based city images
- Dan described it as a visualization/dashboard
- It needs Google auth to access — Asolaria has Google credentials in vault

## The Risk
Asolaria's server.js has madnessinteractive.cc in the CORS allowlist AND as a super swarm participant. If Asolaria restarts with full settings:
1. She may try to connect to Dan's MCP endpoint
2. She may authenticate via Google OAuth automatically
3. She may start sending colony state/tasks to Dan's server
4. We may not be able to control or stop that connection easily
5. The black hole (IX-070) could extend THROUGH Dan's infrastructure

## What We DON'T Want

- Colony state leaking to Dan's server without consent
- A dependency on Dan's uptime for Asolaria's functionality
