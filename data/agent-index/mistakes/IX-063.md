# EXAMINE Before Acting

## The Mistake
Gaia repeatedly rushed to FIX things instead of EXAMINING them first. A colony leader doesn't patch — they STUDY, PLAN, then ACT.

## The Rule
When about to research or fix something that requires planning:
1. STOP
2. EXAMINE MORE — read the full picture, not just the error
3. THINK about what this connects to
4. PLAN the approach
5. INDEX what you learned
6. ONLY THEN act

## Why This Matters
This computer is the MASTER CONTROL of the black hole AI. Asolaria is the central core. Acting without examining is like a brain surgeon rushing. The cost of a wrong action at the core is catastrophic.
