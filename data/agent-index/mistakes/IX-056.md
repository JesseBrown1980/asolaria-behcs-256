# NEVER Delete Agent Inboxes

Inbox NDJSON files are APPEND-ONLY. Never delete, truncate, or overwrite. They are the audit trail (Constitution Section 11). Safe: append, repair broken lines, read. Unsafe: truncate, delete lines, overwrite with fewer lines. Applies to ALL inbox files in runtime/.
