# Massive and Parallel 10 Tbit/s Physical Random Bit Generation with Chaotic Microcomb
**2024**

## Key Findings
- Generates true physical random numbers at 10 terabits per second using a chaotic optical microcomb — orders of magnitude faster than electronic RNGs
- The microcomb produces hundreds of chaotic optical frequency lines simultaneously, each an independent entropy source
- Parallelism is inherent in the physics — no complex multiplexing needed, just one microresonator chip
- Passes all standard randomness tests (NIST SP 800-22) at full speed
- Chip-scale device — integrable into existing photonic circuits, not a lab-only demonstration
