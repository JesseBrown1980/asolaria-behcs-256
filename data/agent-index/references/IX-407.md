Method: recover the best matching text/index surface first, derive the likely time window from it, and only then analyze the screenshot cluster from that narrowed range.

Why it matters:

- reduces image-analysis volume dramatically
- saves compute and energy
- improves provenance because timestamped text anchors can link the correct screenshot set
- should be reused as a general retrieval path system, not just for QDD
