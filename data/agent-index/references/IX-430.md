Significance of the March 29 internal scan:

- the NovaLUM lane is no longer blocked by USB discovery, COM ownership, or missing UI
- the desktop app can detect the device on `COM9` through the `NL2 INTERFACE ADAPTER`
- the app can switch the device into binary mode and read live globals
- the device-configured sync target is confirmed as `192.168.1.43:443`

Operational consequence:

- the current NovaLUM blocker class has shifted to network reachability and fixture topology
- future work should focus on SSID, LAN reachability, VPN state, and sync-server visibility
- do not start by changing device encryption
- use the desktop sync app as the canonical control surface
