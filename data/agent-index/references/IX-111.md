# Alternate EoS for Finite Density Black Holes in a Quantum Gravity Framework

Companion paper to IX-110. Located in Plankscale Timecrystal Blackhole compression folder.

## Key Findings
- Presents alternative equations of state for finite-density black hole interiors
- Introduces anisotropic pressure profiles (radial vs tangential pressures differ)
- Includes scalar field and potential analysis for black hole core modeling
- Validated numerically with energy density profiles and pressure anisotropy plots
- Extends the original framework with additional boundary conditions and parameters (Wr, Wt, R0, N)
