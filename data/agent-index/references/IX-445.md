## Why it matters

This gives QDD a real historical baseline for comparison against the March 29 live failure.

The current live state is not an unknown architecture problem. The old success log proves:

- the dashboard and sync flow are valid
- the proxy model is valid
- server-side encrypted traffic plus device-side unencrypted traffic is valid
- a completed clean sync used to happen through this app family
