# Provenance-Gated Transport Canon

## Durable Truth

The repeated `18886` drift shows that transport facts cannot be stored as plain statements without provenance.

Minimum fields before promotion:

- `source_kind`
- `host_scope`
- `observed_at`
- `verifier`
- `status`

## Use Rule

If a transport fact comes from documents or historical transcripts without live verification, keep it in draft or mark it as myth/proposed/superseded rather than promoting it as stable canon.
